Illustrating Impact

Sustainable Development Goals

Project:

The aim to further conceptualise and develop a prototype platform – called the ‘External Rate of Return’ – for transforming the way that the “social impact” of business is measured, and crucially, how that information is disseminated across society, as a holistic, inclusive and transparent platform where society-at-large engages in essential communication and analysis of business activities globally. Reducing the incoherence and overlap of current efforts to measure social impact and to demonstrate progress towards achieving the Sustainable Development Goals.
