eEditions

Interface Design

Project:

When I was working at Trinity Mirror, now known as Reach, the Mirror newspaper app for tablets, eEditions, was launched and the interface design was later required to be updated.

Process:

I worked with a product manager to find out what the requirements were and designed the navigation icons, app icons, moved on to help screens, subscription pages, and more in Illustrator, passing on the assests to the development team and tested out the elements in the app during the development phase until the live release.
