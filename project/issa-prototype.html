Source film
Coals to Newcastle — A Story of Recovery
NCB Film Unit, 1947 · Director: J. Eldridge · Commissioned by National Coal Board
A promotional documentary celebrating post-nationalisation recovery in the coal industry. The film presents the 1947 period as one of renewed optimism and worker solidarity under public ownership. The sequence containing your selected clip was originally used to show the hardship that preceded nationalisation — immediately followed by scenes of improvement and progress.
⚠ Reuse flag: This clip's original function was to show hardship as a contrast to subsequent recovery. Used in isolation or without context, it may misrepresent the mood or intent of the people depicted — who in this film are ultimately presented as beneficiaries of nationalisation, not its victims.
