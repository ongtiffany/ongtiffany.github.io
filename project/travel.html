William and Kate's Royal Tour

Travel Itinerary

Project:

Editors at Trinity Mirror, now known as Reach wanted a visual way to show Prince William and Kate Middleton's itinerary when they went on their Austrialia and New Zealand tour.

Process:

A simple slideshow was created using the old fashion gif method in Photoshop as there was no need to implement anything more complicated since they didn't want navigation of controls.
