Water Consumption

Environment Awareness

Project:

A personal project based on the very long showers my kids take. I wanted a way to explain to them how important water was and should be used sparingly but also in a very simplified way that they could relate to and empathise, so a lot might not be completely accurate.

Process:

I took the things they were most interested with at the time, and asked them to depict the few ways they used water. Photographed their creations and collaged them together with some numbers and ratios they could relate to and applied in their lives.
